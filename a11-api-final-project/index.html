Why Should You Be Aware?

Due to the COVID-19 outbreak by the end of 2019, the whole world was greatly affected. The economy went down. Many companies and businesses broke down, especially aviation and tourism. Scarcity of resources and other various problems have risen, it changed all people's way of life. But there is also one thing that we should watch and be aware about, our mental health.

Almost all people focused on how to rise up and survive. But little did they know, these people or the ones around them or close to them can be or were already mentally struggling. They don't have that much time to be aware of their mental health and just tend to put it aside, how much more if we neglected those people who were already mentally struggling even before the pandemic started. And that is the worst thing that can happen. Fear, worry, anxiety, and stress, those things are normal reponses during this time especially of what we are hearing from the news everyday and we should know how to properly cope with these things up. We should also know how to properly assess ourselves or the ones around us of what we all needed mentally. We should properly understand the significance of mental health awareness and especially the mentally struggled ones already close to us that we should care for them.
